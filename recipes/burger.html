Burger

Description

A classic beef burger is a savory delight made with a seasoned ground beef patty, perfectly
grilled or pan-fried to your desired level of doneness. Served on a toasted bun and
garnished with your favorite toppings, it's a timeless comfort food that satisfies
with every bite.

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Toppings of your choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill
  2. Prepare the Meat
  3. Shape the Patties
  4. Cook the Patties
  5. Toast Buns
  6. Assemble Burger
  7. Enjoy!
