Pasta

Description

Classic pasta with tomato sauce is a comforting and timeless dish that features
al dente pasta coated in a rich, flavorful tomato sauce. The pasta is cooked to
perfection, and the sauce is infused with aromatic garlic and herbs. Served piping
hot, it's a simple yet satisfying meal that can be customized with your favorite toppings and garnishes.

Ingrediants

  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) of your favorite pasta (e.g., spaghetti, fettuccine, penne)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
